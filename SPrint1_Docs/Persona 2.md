Persona 2: Record Collector & Music Enthusiast
Name: Reggie Diesel
Age: 28
Status: Music enthusiast / part-time worker


BACKGROUND
Reggie is a music enthusiast who collects a variety of vinyl records as a hobby. He is a interested in swapping them rather than selling them. Sam enjoys discovering new music through community recommendations and forums online.
 

GOALS
• Swap vinyl records with other users
• Discover new music and artists
• Trade records without spending money
	
FRUSTRATIONS/PAINT POINTS
 Selling records online is time-consuming
 Record condition is often unclear on existing platforms
 Worries about dishonest users or damaged items

Trust & Safety Concerns

•	Concerned about users misrepresenting record condition 
•	Wants clear descriptions and honest listings
•	Prefers basic moderation to prevent scams

How this persona would use this application:

• Creates a user profile
• Upload records with condition details
• Browse available records by genre
• Express their interest in swapping with other users
